The number of farmers who desires to grow new crops is increasing. Certain crops that grow in specific regions, states, countries, and other locations can be cultivated in other locations with similar features such as soil type, rainfall patterns, and climatic conditions. Prediction algorithm, k-nearest neighbors(knn) is used to analyze the crop data set and discover such places. This will result in reduced cost of agriculture products as there will be great increase in production
